# Trello-Style Task Manager

A simple **Trello-style Task Manager** built with **React** and **Tailwind CSS**.  
Organize tasks with drag-and-drop columns and manage them easily.

### 1. **Sign Up**
- Click on the **Sign Up** button on the login page.
- Enter a **username** and **password**.
- Click **Sign Up** to create your account.
- Your account is stored **locally in the browser**.

### 2. **Login**
- Enter your **username** and **password** created during signup.
- Click **Login** to access the dashboard.
- If credentials are incorrect, an alert will notify you.

### 3. **Dashboard**
- The dashboard displays three columns:
  - **To-Do**
  - **In-Progress**
  - **Completed**
- All tasks are displayed in these columns according to their status.
- The **current date** is shown at the top.

### 4. **Add a Task**

- The task will appear in the chosen column.

### 5. **Edit a Task**
- Click the **Edit** button on any task.
- Update the task details in the modal.
- Click **Update** to save changes.
- Task will update in the column accordingly.

### 6. **Delete a Task**
- Click the **Delete** button on any task.
- The task will be removed permanently.

### 7. **Drag & Drop**
- Click and drag a task to move it between columns.
- Drop it in the desired column to change its status.

### 8. **Logout**
- Click the **Logout** button in the navbar to end your session.
